ABOUT

I cherish my relationships with my clients and believe in creating spaces that reflect their lifestyles—where they can come home to a space that is truly unique to them. I love getting to know my clients and their families and, at the end of the day, am proud to call them friends.

As a 5th generation Sacramentan, I have deep roots in this city we call home. When I’m not designing, I’m usually with my fur-child, a Great Pyrenees named Eleanor, hanging out with friends, volunteering at Shriners Hospital, wine tasting and I do try to get an occasional trip in, here and there.

EXPERIENCE

After graduating with a BA in Interior Design in 2000, I have concentrated on residential design. In recent years, I’ve added lighting design in both residential and restaurants to my bag of tricks.

I love mixing modern with traditional—with an occasional dose of whimsy, of course—a combination that makes its way into most of my designs and, when mixed with texture and color, make the most approachable and beautiful spaces.
